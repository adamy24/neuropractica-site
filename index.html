Philosophy

Built on How Experts Actually Think

Not flashcards. Not question banks. A reasoning framework that mirrors how seasoned neurologists approach every patient.

Decision-Based Cases

Every case unfolds through your clinical decisions — history, exam, diagnostics, diagnosis, and management — with feedback at each step.

Immediate Feedback

No waiting for answer keys. Get principle-based expert reasoning after every decision — understand not just what, but why.

Adaptive Personalization

Your daily case is selected based on your specific weaknesses — subspecialty gaps, reasoning patterns, and safety-critical areas.

How It Works

Ten Minutes to Sharper Reasoning

Each case follows the framework real neurologists use — chronicity, localization, syndrome recognition, and differential diagnosis.

01

Chief Complaint

A patient presents. You get the clinical scenario and begin forming your approach.

02

Make Decisions

At each step — history, exam, diagnostics — you choose what to do next and get immediate feedback.

03

Commit & Learn

Reach your final diagnosis and management plan. Review expert reasoning and see where your thinking diverged.

04

Get Stronger

Your next case adapts to target the gaps this one revealed. Deliberate practice, not random repetition.
